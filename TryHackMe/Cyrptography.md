# Overview

Cryptography is the practice of securing information by transforming readable data into an unreadable format. It plays a critical role in protecting sensitive information, verifying identities, and ensuring secure communication over networks.

# Learning Objectives

---

# Key Concepts

## Encryption

Encryption converts plaintext into ciphertext so that only authorized users can access the original information.

### Types of Encryption

- Symmetric Encryption
- Asymmetric Encryption

### Hashing

Hashing generates a fixed-length output used to verify data integrity.

Common hashing algorithms include:

- MD5
- SHA-1
- SHA-256
- SHA-512

### Digital Signatures

Digital signatures verify authenticity and ensure that data has not been modified.

### Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)

PKI manages digital certificates and public keys to establish trust between systems.
